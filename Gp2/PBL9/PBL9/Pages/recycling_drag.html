Many parks don’t clearly explain which waste belongs in which bin. Adults may figure it out, but children often find it confusing. As a result, trash gets tossed anywhere, making our parks messy and unsafe.

Our interactive recycling game teaches both kids and adults to sort waste the right way. Using simple drag-and-drop play, they learn which items belong in each bin. It's fun, engaging, and helps build real-life recycling habits that keep parks clean and revitalized.
