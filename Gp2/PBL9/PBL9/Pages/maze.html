Green Maze Challenge

Navigate through the calming pastel labyrinth

Welcome to a serene maze adventure! Your mission is to navigate through the gentle pathways from start to finish. Take your time and enjoy the journey through this beautifully designed, calming labyrinth.

How to Play

  • Objective: Guide the player through the winding paths to reach the goal and complete the maze.
  • Controls: Use the Arrow Keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) on your keyboard to move the character.
  • Start Game: Click the Green Flag on the game screen below to begin your journey.
  • Reset: Hit the Green Flag again if you need to restart from the beginning.
  • Hint: Take it slow and steady - there's no time limit! Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.
